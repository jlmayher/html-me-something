Why Prepare?

Watching the news, it seems like threats are coming from everywhere. Preparing for disasters both natural and man-made is one of the most important tasks for any family. Preparing yourself and your family to react quickly in case of an emergency can ensure the safety and possibly the very survival of your loved ones.
